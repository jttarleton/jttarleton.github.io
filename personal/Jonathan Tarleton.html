Jonathan Tarleton : About & Contact

I’m a student of urban planning, researcher, freelance writer, and oral historian currently living in Somerville, Mass. with roots in the South. I served as the chief researcher and a contributor for Nonstop Metropolis: A New York City Atlas, an investigation of the city through original maps and essays published in October 2016 from University of California Press and editors Rebecca Solnit and Joshua Jelly-Schapiro.

Until recently I edited Urban Omnibus, a magazine of the Architectural League covering planning, policy, architecture, art, and activism in New York City. While there, I also edited a book collecting the winners of the annual Omnibus writing competition—get yours here.
